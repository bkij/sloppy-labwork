About Deck Registration

Many online tournaments require that aplayers prove they own a deck before playing with it in an event. Usually this involves taking a picture of your deck's archon card alongside a handwritten note stating who the deck belongs to.

The registration service here aims to cerntalize that process so players only have to prove they own a deck once, and tournmanet organizers don't have to worry about dealing with verification pictures.

How It Works, For Players

Registering a deck with Sloppy Labwork is similar to verifying ownership for a typical online event. After creating an account, you can submit a registration request here. Your registration request will include a photo of your archon card, as well as a handwritten note containing a randomly generated 5-digit code.

From there, a Sloppy Labwork administartor will review the request. Once it is approved, the deck will be publicly registered to you.

Keep in mind that after verification, your photo will be visible to those who would like to double check your claim of ownership. Make sure to cover your deck's QR code when taking a photo. Registration requests with a fully visible QR code will be declined.
