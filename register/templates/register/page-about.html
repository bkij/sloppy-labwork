About Deck Registration

Many online tournaments require that aplayers prove they own the deck before playing with it in an event. Usually this means taking a picture of your deck's archon card alongside a handwritten note stating who the deck belongs to.

The registration service here aims to cerntalize that process so players only have to prove they own a deck once, and tournmanet organizers don't have to worry about juggling verification pictures.

How It Works, For Players

Registering a deck with Sloppy Labwork is just like verifying ownership for a typical online event. After creating an account, you can submit a registration request here. Your registration request will include a photo of the back of your archon card, as well as a handwritten note listing your usernames on relevant online platforms.

From there, a Sloppy Labwork administartor will review the request. Once it is approved, the deck will be pubicly registered to you.
